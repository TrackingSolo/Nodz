What Nodz Is

Nodz is an Android app that wakes you at the optimal point in your sleep cycle — during light sleep — so you feel refreshed instead of groggy. Rather than firing at a fixed time, Nodz monitors your sleep in real time and picks the best moment within your chosen wake window.

  • Real-time sleep monitoring via Android Health Connect — reads sleep stages, HRV, and movement data directly from your device.
  • Predictive sleep algorithm as a fallback — uses 90-minute cycle math combined with your personal calibration history when sensor data is unavailable.
  • Spotify wake music — starts your chosen playlist and ramps volume from 10% to 100% for a gentle rise. Falls back to your Android system ringtone if Spotify is unavailable.
  • AlarmManager failsafe — if no smart-wake trigger fires, a final alarm sounds at your scheduled time so you never oversleep.
  • Oura Ring integration — connect via Personal Access Token for richer sleep metrics including readiness score and detailed HRV.
  • Sleep Number integration — connects to your SleepIQ account via API for bed-sensor data.
  • Garmin Connect integration Coming Soon — Garmin device support is in development.
How the wake window works: You set a wake-up target time. Nodz opens a 30-minute window before that time and watches for light sleep. The moment it detects the right stage, it wakes you — up to 30 minutes early if that means a smoother morning.

Setup & Use

Getting started takes about five minutes. Follow these steps once and Nodz handles the rest each night.

  1. 1
    Grant Health Connect permissions
    When prompted, approve access to sleep stages, heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), and activity recognition. Nodz uses these to detect your sleep onset and identify light-sleep wake moments.
  2. 2
    Set a wake-up alarm
    On the main screen, tap the + button. Enter your target wake time. Nodz will open a 30-minute smart-wake window before that time automatically.
  3. 3
    Connect your integrations
    Go to Settings → Integrations and connect any devices you use:
    • Health Connect — automatically detected if installed on your device.
    • Oura Ring — enter your Personal Access Token from cloud.ouraring.com/personal-access-tokens.
    • Sleep Number — enter your SleepIQ email address and password.
  4. 4
    Connect Spotify (optional)
    In Settings, tap Connect Spotify and sign in. Then choose a wake playlist. Nodz will start this playlist at low volume and gradually increase it when your wake moment arrives.
  5. 5
    Tap "Start Bedtime" when you're ready to sleep
    The app enters Lullaby mode — a dark, distraction-free screen. Nodz listens quietly for sleep onset in the background.
What happens while you sleep
  • ▸ Lullaby mode — dark screen; app listens for sleep onset signal.
  • ▸ Monitoring mode — once sleep is confirmed, Nodz records your cycle anchor time and begins adaptive polling.
  • ▸ Wake window — 30 minutes before your alarm, Nodz checks for a light sleep stage every 1–2 minutes.
  • ▸ Smart wake — light sleep detected? Spotify starts, volume ramps from 10% → 100%.
  • ▸ Failsafe — no smart-wake trigger? AlarmManager fires at alarm time minus 3 minutes.

Viewing Your Sleep Results

After each night, open the Dashboard screen to review your sleep data. The Dashboard shows your full history and improves its accuracy as it learns your personal sleep patterns over time.

  • Sleep history — a chronological log of every tracked night, sorted by date.
  • Total sleep — total hours and minutes of confirmed sleep per night.
  • Sleep stages breakdown — proportion of time spent in light, REM, deep, and awake stages.
  • HRV — heart rate variability readings for each night (requires Health Connect or Oura Ring).
  • Readiness score — Oura-sourced readiness score when Oura Ring is connected.
  • Cycle count — number of full 90-minute sleep cycles completed.
